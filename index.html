What is Study Buddy?

In today's fast-paced world it can be stressful for children to stay on top of their chores when technology keeps them distracted. Enter Study Buddy; the App that helps children stay focused by making there to-do list an enjoyable, game like experience! Parents set tasks for their children and can set the rewards as well as block the apps they don't want to distract their children to keep them on task. Once the child completes their task they will receive an in-app currency to spend on their own spaces cosmetics! Avaialable on iOS and Android Now!
